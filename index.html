Problems I Solve

I help small businesses solve practical technical and data-related problems that affect visibility, usability, reporting, and day-to-day operations.

Dashboard visuals are confusing

Power BI, Tableau, or Excel reports may work technically but still fail to clearly explain what is happening in the business.

Reports have blank visuals or incorrect numbers

I help identify broken calculations, data issues, and reporting problems so the final output is accurate and presentation-ready.

Website forms are not submitting properly

If users are filling out forms but requests are not coming through, I can review the issue and help pinpoint what is failing.

Users cannot access features or pages

I troubleshoot access issues, broken pages, and feature problems that affect the customer or user experience.

Businesses need dashboards built quickly

For meetings, presentations, or stakeholder reviews, I can help turn business data into a cleaner, more usable reporting view.

Apps or websites need a better user experience

If users are saying a system feels confusing or difficult to use, I can help review usability problems and improvement opportunities.

Workflows are too manual and inefficient

I help businesses think through simple tools, dashboards, and process improvements that save time and reduce confusion.

Business owners have data but not clarity

Having data is not enough. I help transform raw information into something decision-makers can actually understand and use.

History

Gwin Analytics grew out of hands-on experience in technical support, ticket-based workflows, troubleshooting, and business operations. Over time, that foundation expanded into dashboard development and analytics work focused on helping businesses solve problems and better understand performance.

Today, the platform serves as both a portfolio of practical work and a structured consulting system for small businesses that need technical support, reporting clarity, and usable insights.
